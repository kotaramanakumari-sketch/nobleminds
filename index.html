✦ Features

Everything you need to manage student records

From student enrollment to parent details, medical history, and identity verification — all in one secure platform.

🏫
Multi-School Management

Register multiple schools and assign dedicated staff users. Each school manages its own students independently.

👤
Complete Student Profiles

Capture student photo, admission number, class, section, house, DOB, gender, religion, caste, and full address details.

👨‍👩‍👧
Parent Information

Maintain detailed father and mother profiles with photos, qualification, occupation, and contact information.

🆔
Identity Documents

Securely store Aadhar number, APAAR number, and PEN for every student for government compliance.

🏥
Medical History

Record and track student medical history for counselling support and emergency preparedness.

🏆
Extracurricular Tracking

Track participation in NCC, NSS, SGFI, and Scouts & Guides activities for each student.

📥
Bulk Import via CSV

Import hundreds of student records at once using our CSV template. Preview before saving.

🔐
Role-Based Access

Admin controls schools and users. School staff only access their own school's student data.

📊
Smart Dashboard

Instant overview of student counts, gender distribution, class-wise breakdown, and activity stats.

✦ Process

How NobleMinds works

Simple 4-step process to get your school up and running.

1
Admin Registers School

Super Admin registers a new school with its details and code.

2
Create School Users

Admin creates user accounts for school staff and assigns them to their school.

3
Staff Adds Students

School staff logs in and adds student records individually or via bulk CSV import.

4
Access Anywhere

View, filter, search, and manage all student records from any device, anytime.
